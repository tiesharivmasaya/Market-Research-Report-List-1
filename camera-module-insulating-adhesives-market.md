<p><p>The camera module insulating adhesives market is categorized into two main types: UV curable type and non-curable type. UV curable adhesives are activated by ultraviolet light, allowing for quick curing and strong bonding, which is ideal for high-speed production environments. In contrast, non-curable adhesives rely on conventional chemical curing processes, offering versatility for various applications but generally requiring longer drying times. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring the performance and durability of camera modules in electronic devices.</p></p>
