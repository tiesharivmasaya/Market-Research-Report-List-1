<p><p>The industrial grade soda ash market is primarily categorized into two types: dense soda ash and light soda ash. Dense soda ash is characterized by its higher bulk density, making it suitable for various applications, including glass manufacturing and chemical production, where space optimization is crucial. In contrast, light soda ash has a lower bulk density and is often used in applications requiring quicker dissolution, such as detergents and water treatment. Both types serve essential roles across multiple industries.</p></p>
