<p><p>The ballistic security glass market encompasses various types, each designed for specific protective needs. Traditional laminated glass consists of layers that absorb impact, while insulated ballistic glass offers enhanced thermal efficiency alongside ballistic protection. Acrylic is lightweight and offers good resistance but can scratch easily. Polycarbonate is known for its high impact resistance and durability. Lastly, glass-clad polycarbonate combines layers of glass and polycarbonate, providing superior protection while maintaining visibility, making it suitable for high-security applications.</p></p>
